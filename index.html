MedEar — CareCaller AI
Voice Agent

Telephony audio at 8kHz mangles drug names. A transcription error in a clinical call isn't a typo — it's a medication error. MedEar is a three-layer pipeline: fine-tuned Whisper ASR, a phonetic correction engine covering 321 real drug mispronunciations from the top 500 US prescriptions, and medical NER that extracts structured JSON.

About Me

I started as a software engineer at Sonata Software, working with enterprise ERP systems across 200+ clients — and learned early that the most valuable skill isn't building models. It's finding patterns in messy operational data that nobody else has spotted yet.

That instinct carried into graduate school at ASU, where I built a solar forecasting model that fueled further lab research, automated faculty reporting systems, and built MedEar — a clinical AI voice agent solving a real problem: drug names getting mangled on 8kHz telephony audio in ways that cause medication errors.

I build things that work in production. I document them so others can use them. And I care about whether the end result actually helps someone.
